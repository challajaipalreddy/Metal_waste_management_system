Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Metal Exchange?

Simply create an account by choosing your role (seller, processor, or buyer), fill in your details, and you're ready to go! Our team will guide you through the process.

What types of metal waste do you accept?

We accept all types of metal waste including steel, aluminum, copper, brass, and other non-ferrous metals. Each type is processed according to industry standards.

How is the payment processed?

Payments are processed instantly through our secure platform. You can choose to receive payment via bank transfer, UPI, or our digital wallet.
